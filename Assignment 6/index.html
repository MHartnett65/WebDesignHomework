Frybread

Cooked with generational love.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Gradually add warm water and mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth.
  4. Divide the dough into golf ball-sized portions and flatten each into a disc.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or fryer over medium-high heat.
  6. Fry each dough disc until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Remove from the oil and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy! Tastes really good with soup and chili.
