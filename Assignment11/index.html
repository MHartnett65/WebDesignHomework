The Medicine Wheel is a sacred symbol used by many Native American cultures, particularly those of the Plains tribes. It represents the interconnectedness of all things and serves as a guide for living a balanced and harmonious life. It is also a belief in how I was taught is it represents all the knowledge on mother earth. We strive to learn and become better from the gifts of mother earth.

The four quadrants of the Medicine Wheel symbolize various aspects of existence:

  • The East represents the beginning, birth, and heart.
  • The South represents growth, youth, and soul.
  • The West represents adulthood, introspection, and body.
  • The North represents wisdom, elderhood, completion, and Mind.

Additionally, the Medicine Wheel embodies the interconnectedness of all living beings and the natural world. It teaches respect for all life forms and encourages harmony with nature.
